Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Spanish Step ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Quick Summary: Spanish Step is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our vocabulary learning service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Learning Progress: Your vocabulary study progress, bookmarked words, and quiz scores are stored locally on your device
  • Preferences: App settings such as language preference, dark mode, and notification settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance

3. Advertising

Spanish Step uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data for personalized advertising purposes.

  • Free users can study up to 30 words per day
  • After reaching the daily limit, you can watch a rewarded video ad to unlock unlimited access until midnight
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • For more information, please visit Google's Privacy Policy

4. In-App Purchases

Spanish Step offers in-app purchases to remove advertisements. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

5. Data Storage

Your learning data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal study data to external servers. If you delete the app, your local data will be removed.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Apple/Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics

7. Children's Privacy

Spanish Step is suitable for all ages (4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed as an educational tool for Spanish language learning.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored within the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
