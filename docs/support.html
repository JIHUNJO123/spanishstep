How many words are included in the app?
Spanish Step includes 5,000 carefully curated vocabulary words across four DELE levels: A1 (600 words), A2 (600 words), B1 (1,300 words), and B2 (2,500 words).

Can I use the app offline?
Yes! All vocabulary content is stored locally on your device. You can study anywhere without an internet connection.
How do I remove ads and daily limits?
You can remove all advertisements and unlock unlimited daily access by purchasing the "Remove Ads" option for $1.99 from the Settings menu within the app.
How many words can I study for free?
Free users can study up to 30 words per day. After reaching the daily limit, you can watch a short rewarded video ad to unlock unlimited access until midnight (local time). The limit resets at midnight every day.
What are rewarded video ads?
Rewarded ads are short video advertisements (15-30 seconds). When you watch one after hitting the daily limit, you get unlimited word access until midnight. They are completely optional.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Go to Settings > Restore Purchases in the app. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.
Is my progress saved?
Yes, your learning progress is automatically saved on your device. Note that if you uninstall the app, your progress will be lost as data is stored locally.
What DELE level should I start with?
If you're a complete beginner, start with A1. If you have some basic knowledge, try A2. The app will track your progress and you can advance at your own pace.
